Search engines rank websites using factors such as relevance, quality, user experience, and technical health. Relevance ensures content matches search intent. Technical health covers site structure, security, and crawlability. BS Digital Solutions aligns SEO initiatives with these criteria to ensure compliance, performance, and scalability.
