Designing compassionate best mental health care branding with strategic clarity, trust, empathy, and meaningful human connection

Client: Ollato
Industry Category: Mental Health Wellness 
Nature of the Project: Rebranding 
Services: Brand Strategy, Logo Creation, Visual Identity, Website, and Social Media Marketing.

Client Briefing:

The client wants a full rebrand, beginning with a new logo that gives a fresh, modern feel. This project also covers creating a new brand strategy, positioning, and voice to align with their evolving business goals. The goal is to build a strong, unified brand identity that connects with their audience and helps them stand out in the market.

Our Creative Approach:

Logo Design Inspiration:

The Ollato identity is founded on the metaphor of transformation and human connection. The butterfly symbolizes emotional growth and mental evolution, reflecting the journey from inner struggle to well-being. Each wing forms a human profile facing inward, representing the empathetic dialogue between client and counselor, where understanding, trust, and healing begin. The subtle central head in negative space highlights the mind as the brand’s core focus.

Color Psychology:

The blue-green gradient carries emotional meaning. Teal evokes healing, openness, and renewal, while deep blue communicates stability, trust, and professionalism. The smooth transition mirrors the counseling journey, which is guided, calm, and transformative.

Typeface:

The lowercase “ollato” wordmark conveys warmth, accessibility, and approachability, which are essential for a mental health wellness brand. Rounded letterforms reflect the organic flow of the symbol and create visual harmony.

Conceptual Summary:

Ollato’s identity combines sensitivity and professionalism, showing what mental health and wellness mean through its design. The rounded lowercase wordmark feels warm, open, and friendly, while the symbol represents empathy, trust, and personal change. This mix of care and credibility helps the brand’s visual identity connect with its mission of healing.
