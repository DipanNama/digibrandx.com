Looking for the best fintech branding with a great visual identity? We craft strategic, impactful, high-growth brands.

Client: OneBhrat
Industry Category: Fintech 
Nature of the Project: Branding 
Services: Brand Strategy, Logo Creation, Visual Identity, Website, Digital Marketing, and Brand Film.

Client Briefing:

ONEBHARAT is a fintech brand that offers credit card swipe machines for today’s Indian consumer. The brand believes in simplicity, which shows in its minimal logo and design. OneBharat aims to support Indian middle-class women who use credit cards for daily shopping by making payments easier and more secure. The company focuses on trust, affordability, and accessibility, aiming to be a reliable partner for both small businesses and individual shoppers. Its identity is proudly Indian, created to meet the changing needs of the local market.

Our Creative Approach:

OneBharat is a fintech brand created for today’s Indian consumer, with a focus on credit card swipe machines. The main audience is Indian middle-class women who often use credit cards for shopping. The logo and design draw on Indian culture and the energy of festivals, combining simple design with meaningful symbols to create a strong, memorable identity.

Logo Design Inspiration:

The OneBharat logo is simple but meaningful, using two circles to represent debit and credit in the transaction process. The link between the circles shows the flow of money and the smooth experience OneBharat aims to provide. These circles also have significance in Indian culture, where they represent balance and prosperity.

Color Psychology:

The logo uses green and blue. Green stands for growth, prosperity, and harmony, which match OneBharat’s values. Blue represents trust, security, and reliability. These colors help build brand confidence and make customers feel safe during transactions. The bright colors also capture the lively spirit of Indian festivals, helping the brand connect with its audience.

Typeface:

The logo uses a clean, modern, and simple typeface to make it easy to read. This choice highlights the brand’s focus on simplicity and user-friendliness, which is important to the target audience, especially those not used to complex technology.

Conceptual Summary:

OneBharat’s design balances simple style with meaningful symbols, showing both the practical side of financial transactions and India’s rich culture. With its logo, colors, and typeface, the brand aims to connect with modern, middle-class Indian women by building trust, reliability, and a sense of cultural belonging.
